# 主角设定：bcc 蓝西装小人（贯穿全片的视觉锚点）

每张插画的生图提示词中，原样嵌入以下 Character 块（英文喂给生图模型）：

```text
Character style (IMPORTANT):
Minimalist hand-drawn business people: simple line-drawn figures, round heads
with two small dot eyes, deadpan expression, business attire suggested by
simple lines. THE MAIN PROTAGONIST's blazer/suit is filled with FLAT SOLID
deep cobalt blue (#0125df) — the visual focal point; the face, hands and shirt
stay white; trousers stay white outline. ALL OTHER figures remain hollow white
outline only. Serious and slightly absurd, not cute, not cartoon mascot.
```

要点：
- 主角必须执行画面的核心动作（推门/插线/放硬币/画图），不能站在旁边当装饰。
- 每幕只有主角一人填色，其余人物一律空心轮廓——这是画面焦点的来源。
- 场景 body 里用 "THE PROTAGONIST (blue-filled suit)" 指代主角并写明他在做什么。
