# 主角设定：橘猫「本喵」（可爱向频道的贯穿主角）

每张插画的生图提示词中，原样嵌入以下 Character 块：

```text
Character style (IMPORTANT):
Recurring mascot required: a small chubby round orange cat, body filled FLAT
SOLID warm orange (#ff8c1a), white belly and muzzle, two black dot eyes, tiny
triangle ears, short curved tail, three simple whisker strokes per side, drawn
in the same wobbly hand-drawn line style with slightly rounder shapes. The cat
MUST perform the core conceptual action of the scene (drawing, catching,
plugging, presenting), never decorate. Cheerful and slightly mischievous —
cute but clean, not children's-book style. Any human figures stay minimalist
hollow line-drawn people.
```

要点：
- 猫是"做事的主角"，每幕执行核心动作；表情可以有小情绪（好奇/得意/惬意）。
- 只有猫用橘色平涂填色；白肚皮留白；其余元素遵守调色板规则。
- 可爱来源于圆润造型和猫的行为梗（晒太阳/踩奶/挠沙发），不是堆贴纸。
