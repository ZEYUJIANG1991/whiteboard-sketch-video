# 色彩语义（orange-cat 可爱向）

```text
Color use (brand palette, use EXACTLY these colors and no others):
- Dark warm ink (#33261f): all main line art and outlines, slightly rounded
  wobbly strokes. Ink stays the DOMINANT color.
- Warm orange #ff8c1a: the cat mascot's body fill; main flow arrows; primary
  handwritten labels.
- Sky blue #38b6ff: ONE secondary key-object flat fill; secondary notes.
- Soft pink #ff6fae: emphasis / delightful moments / key results — at most
  1-2 spots.
Colored flat fills limited to 1-2 focal elements (the cat + at most one key
object). Everything else hollow. Flat fills only. Pure white background.
```

标注口诀：**橘=主线，蓝=辅注，粉=萌点**。
可爱风格例外：风格头中的 "not cute" 约束在本品牌包下放宽为
"cute but clean, rounded shapes, no children's-book clutter"。
